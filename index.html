The World

The sun has turned.

In a scorched future, unfiltered solar radiation reshapes life at the cellular level. Pockets of survivors huddle in fortified camps, rationing what little shade remains. The Solarium — a towering institutional compound — promises sanctuary, but its walls hide a truth that could unravel everything.

You are a new arrival. An outsider. Armed with nothing but your instincts and the choices you make, you must navigate a world that is equal parts beautiful and lethal.

The Setting

A wilderness on the edge of collapse.

From frost-cracked tundra to sun-bleached canyon flats, every environment in The Solarium breathes tension. The land itself has become hostile — and the creatures within it, more so.

Explore, scavenge, and survive — but every step into the wilds carries consequences you may not see coming.

Branching Narrative

Every word.
Every silence.
Every choice.

The Solarium is built on consequence. Decisions made in the first act ripple into the third in ways you won't expect. There are no clean heroes here — only people trying to survive.
